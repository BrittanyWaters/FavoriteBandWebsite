"2! 3! Hello, we are BTS!"

BTS is a Korean boyband that debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. When they first debuted, Big Hit was a small, struggling entertainment company. But now, it is the most successful entertainment company in South Korea, in large part due to BTS's success. But it wasn't easy for them, they struggled for years to be known, slowly gaining traction in 2015. Every comback after that skyrocketed with even more success than the previous.

The seven members of BTS are (in order of age):

  • Jin
  • SUGA
  • j-hope
  • RM | leader
  • Jimin
  • V
  • Jung Kook

BTS have even made waves in Western media. Winning several awards and preforming at American awards shows. In 2017, they teamed up with UNICEF for their Love Myself campaign against violence toward children and teens around the world, with the hope of making the world a better place through music.
